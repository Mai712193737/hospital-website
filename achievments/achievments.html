Proud Achievements of Our Medical Team

Al-Shifa Hospital is the largest accredited medical facility in the Delta region. The hospital has been awarded the Egyptian Accreditation Certificate (GAHAR) by the General Authority for Healthcare Accreditation and Regulation (GAHAR) in Egypt. It has also achieved the ISO 22000 certification for the third consecutive time, ensuring the provision of safe food for patients and visitors. Al-Shifa Hospital provides all medical specialties to deliver the highest quality healthcare using the latest diagnostic and therapeutic equipment, with a capacity of up to 300 beds. The hospital has 8 operating rooms, allowing for multiple surgeries to be performed simultaneously. These operating rooms are designed using the capsule system, considered the highest global standard for infection control. Additionally, the surgical department is equipped with the latest endoscopic and microscopic surgery devices across various medical specialties, including:
General Surgery
Neurosurgery
Vascular Surgery
Ophthalmic Surgery
ENT Surgery (Ear, Nose & Throat)
Obstetrics and Gynecology Surgery
Maxillofacial Surgery
Orthopedic Surgery
