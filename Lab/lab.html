Types of Lab tests

Full blood test

Comprehensive analysis of your blood that helps evaluate your overall health and detect a wide range of conditions.

Blood sugar test

Measures the amount of glucose in your blood to help diagnose and monitor diabetes and other blood sugar-related conditions.

Kidney functions test

Assesses how well your kidneys are filtering waste and excess fluids from your blood, helping detect kidney disease or dysfunction.

Urine test

Analyzes urine to detect signs of infection, kidney problems, diabetes, and other underlying health issues.

Liver function test

Evaluates the health of your liver by measuring levels of proteins, liver enzymes, and bilirubin in your blood.

Thyroid function test

Checks how well your thyroid is working by measuring hormone levels related to metabolism, energy, and mood.

Test booking steps:

  1. Click on any of the cards
  2. Input your information
  3. Select Lab services
  4. Submit the form

Result duration:

Some tests can be received on the same day of doing them, but some of the more complicated tests take from 24 to 48 hours from the time of taking the test.
